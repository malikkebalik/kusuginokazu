What is Dennis Waterman's Net Worth?

Dennis Waterman was an English actor and singer who had a net worth of $5 million at the time of his death. Dennis Waterman died on May 8, 2022 at the age of 74. Dennis Waterman was born in Clapham, London, England in February 1948. He was known for his tough guy roles and has starred in film, television, and theater. In 1962 he starred as William Brown in the television series William. In 1962 Waterman starred as Neville Finch in the TV series Fair Exchange and in 1964 he starred as Mike in the series The Barnstormers. Waterman starred as Det. Sgt. George Carter in the television series The Sweeney from 1975 to 1978. He starred as Terry in the TV series Minder from 1979 to 1989. Waterman starred as Tony Carpenter on the series On the Up from 1990 to 1992 and as Thomas Gynn on the series Stay Lucky from 1989 to 1993. Since 2003 Waterman has starred as Gerry Standing on the television series New Tricks. He has starred in the movies The Pirates of Blood River, Scars of Dracula, Alice's Adventures in Wonderland, A Captain's Tale, and Back in Business. As a singer Waterman has released the albums Down Wind Of Angels, Waterman, and So Good For You.
