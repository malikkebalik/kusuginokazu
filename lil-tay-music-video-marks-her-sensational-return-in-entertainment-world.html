Lil Tay Music Video Marks Her Sensational Return in Entertainment World

Tay Tian often known as Lil Tay is a Canadian internet sensation and singer who was born in the United States on July 29, 2007. She rose to fame online for three months in 2018 by identifying herself as the “youngest flexer of the century.”

She posted rap videos on social media during her brief career and they received tens of millions of views. After her father requested full custody and management of her job in a Canadian superior court application, her career came to a stop in the middle of 2018.

The young YouTuber made a comeback to social media with brand-new music and a music video.

What is the Name of Lil Tay’s Music Video?

The teenage YouTuber returned to the platform with a music video for her new song called “Sucker 4 Green” on September 30, 2023. Lil Tay captioned the video on Instagram, “IM BACK”

The rapper-influencer dances in skimpy attire on a mansion’s balcony and inside a garage full of expensive cars in the video to convey her love of money. Lil Tay is seen throwing money into the air at the end of the video while posing with her mother Angela Tian and older brother Jason Tian.

In contrast to her prior rap persona, Lil Tay’s new song is a pop tune. She displays her desire for money in the song’s lyrics, singing in the chorus:

“Money, money, money / Money, money, money / I just can’t look away from it, I want it, want it, want it.”

She flaunts her wealth by parking expensive cars in a garage and shooting all around a home as an homage to her previous viral films. Lil Tay, her mother Angela Tian and her brother Jason Tian sprinkle cash from a balcony to signify the end of the video.

In a pinned comment that was placed beneath her YouTube video, Lil Tay said, “THE BIGGEST COMEBACK OF THE CENTURY.”

Lil Tay proceeded to speak out for the first time the news of her fake death before releasing the “Sucker 4 Green” music video.

How did Lil Tay React to Her Fake Death News?

Her fake death was disclosed on her Instagram profile over two months prior. Her mother’s attorneys confirmed that the young Canadian influencer is alive. Lil Tay’s family informed that her Instagram account had been hacked on August 10.

Lil Tay has returned to the entertainment industry after the resolution of her parents’ child support dispute in August. Her mother’s attorneys gave details in a statement on August 18. You can read their statement in a post which is provided below.

Family lawyers Lorne MacLean, K.C. and Fraser MacLean claim that the Vancouver legal firm MacLean Legal successfully obtained orders for our client that have enabled her daughter to advance her career.

Her mother won from Lil Tay’s father continuous monthly child support as well as retroactive child support for Lil Tay also known as Tay Tian by her mother and attorneys. Angela Tian was given sole day-to-day and final decision-making powers and responsibilities in the best interests of Tay Tian.

In the days before the debut of her music video, the teen’s conflict with her father grew more intense. Lil Tay claimed in an Instagram Story that her father was “abusive” and “faked” her passing.
