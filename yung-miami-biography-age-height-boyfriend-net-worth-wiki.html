Yung Miami Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

Yung Miami is an American Rapper for a duo group named City Girls. She was fond of the hip-hop genre and trap music. Before her music career, she used to be an Instagram influencer and promoted her own fashion line. She suddenly started getting huge attention from the media after Sean Combs AKA Diddy confirmed their relationship publicly.

Early Life

Caresha Romeka Brownlee professionally known as Yung Miami was born on 11th February 1994 in Miami, Florida. The name of her birth parent is Keenya Young. She was later adopted by Trick Daddy and Trina who are rappers. Yung went to Carol Middle School in Miami Gardens, Florida. She has also completed her high school studies but the details about the same have not been shared with the media. We can assume that she must have been trained in the field of the music industry.

Yung Miami Career

In 2017 she and her partner JT came out with a group named City Girl which was later incorporated by Quality Control Music. She released a song named Twerk featuring Cardi B which received a lot of views and appreciation from the public.  Some of her songs in 2017 were Fuck Dat  Nigga, My Neck, My Back with Khia, and some albums named Control the streets Volume 1.

Some of their songs were Act UP, Where the Bag At, Period, You Tried it, JT First Day Out, Money Fight, Kitty Talk, Season, Millionaire Dick, Movie, Like That, No Time, and many more.  The group has 1.57 million subscribers on its YouTube Channel.

Yung will be releasing more songs with her partner and will also collaborate with other popular artists. She updates her fans through her Instagram account. Along with that she has been attending various events and is also busy writing songs.

Controversy

Yung has always dealt with controversies like a professional. Usually, she had a lot of controversy in her personal life, as she keeps her work and personal life separate and away from the public, somehow it was known to the media. She was in an abusive relationship and received sole custody of her children. Like a professional, she deals with criticism in a positive manner. She has received a lot of criticism for not respecting LGBTQ thoughts.

Personal Life

She dated Jai Wiggins for a few years and they were blessed with a son named Jai Malik Wigging Jr. Jai Wiggins was very abusive and has punched her, slapped her, ripped her wig, and shown aggressive behaviors toward her. Eventually, the police were involved and the court gave her sole custody of her child. Later in December 2018, she began dating Southside who is an American record producer.

After a few months, they were separated but the reason is still unknown about the same. In June 2019 she announced her pregnancy and was blessed with a baby girl named Summer Miami Luellen. Recently in June 2021, she was spotted holding hands with Sean Combs professionally known as Diddy who is an American rapper. Recently Diddy even confirmed that they are dating and together go to strip clubs, and church, and even visited exotic locations as well.
