Paolo Bernardini Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Paolo Bernardini is an Italian Actor and Producer. He is well known for his role in shows and films like The Vatican, In Search of Fellini, Francesco, Rosy Abate: La Serie, All the Money in the World, and other projects. Currently, he is in limelight for starring in the movie named The Wedding Veiled Unveiled.

Early Life

Paolo Bernardini was born on 17th December 1981 in Verviers, Belgium. He was born in Belgium but originally hails from Italy. The details about his parents and siblings have not been shared with the media yet. At a young age, he showed interest in an acting career. Paolo has completed his high school education but has not shared details about the same. He further went to Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia to pursue a career in acting. After his studies, he worked on many Italian and international projects.

Paolo Bernardini Career

Paolo was into various theatre projects before getting his first on-screen debut job. In 2007 he worked in projects like Notte Prima degli esami-Oggi, Ho Voglia di te, Scrivilo sui muri and Concrete romance. Next year he was seen in movies and series like Noi due and Ultimi della classes. In 2009 he was seen in series named Distretto di polizia. Next year he worked in Non C’e trmpo per gli eroi. In 2011 he worked in short film named Lucrezia Borgia and a film named Il delitto di via poma. From 2013 to 2016 he worked in many films and series. Some of the projects are titled Like the Wind, The Vatican, Francesco, Erminio Binto, Run from me, E-bola, Bubbly, Chernobyl, and Mannarino- Apriti Cielo.

Next year he was seen in short films and series like The Rope, In search of Fellini, Rosy Abate: La Serie, and All the Money in the World. In 2018 he was seen in a short film named Megan. Next year he worked in Very Valentine as Gianluca Vechiarelli, Il paradiso delle signore as Vincent Defois and L’etoile as Fidanzato. In 2020 he was seen in TV series named Luna Nera. In 2021 he worked in a movie named Lost & Found in Rome in the role of Alex Malvesi. Next year he worked in the film named The Wedding Veil Unveiled as Paolo. Due to the Covid-19 pandemic some of his projects were also postponed. The names of those projects are La Via per Parigi and But You…. Do You Love Me..?
