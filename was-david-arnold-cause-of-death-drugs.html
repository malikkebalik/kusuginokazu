American stand-up comedian, sitcom creator, producer, and actor David A. Arnold died on September 7, 2022. Arnold served as a writer and producer for the Full House series’ Netflix revival.

Just a handful of the humorous shows he worked on include Tyler Perry’s House of Payne (TBS), Tyler Perry’s Bigger (BET+), Meet the Browns (TBS), The Rickey Smiley Show (TV One), Raising Whitley (OWN), and The Tony Rock Project.

In 2021, Arnold served as the sitcom That Girl Lay Lay’s creator and showrunner. He made several TV appearances, including ones in The Tony Rock Project, Meet the Browns, and A Series of Unlucky People.

On Netflix, David A. Arnold’s two stand-up specials Fat Ballerina and It Ain’t For The Weak were both accessible on July 2022. Ashlyn (2007) and Anna-Grace (2005), the couple’s two daughters, were born to Arnold and Julie L. Harkness.
