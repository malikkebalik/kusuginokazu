Child Missing in Kerala: In a harrowing incident that sent shockwaves through the community, six-year-old Abigail Sara Reji was abducted from her home in Oyoor, Kollam district, Kerala. The incident unfolded on November 27, 2023, sparking a massive search operation and concerns among locals. Here is what we know.

Child Missing in Kerala: Child Kidnapped Kerala Kollam:

Abigail Sara Reji was abducted near her residence in Oyoor, Kollam, on November 27. The culprits, four individuals including a woman, took her in a car, initiating a frantic search by the police and the community. The incident raised fears and prompted immediate action to locate and rescue the young girl.

Abigail Sara Abduction Kerala:

The Kerala Police, in their relentless pursuit, managed to trace the details of the car used in the abduction and even created a sketch of one of the kidnappers. This proactive approach by law enforcement played a crucial role in the subsequent events. It is believed that the kidnappers, feeling the pressure from the intensified search efforts, decided to abandon the child at Asramam Maidan before fleeing the scene.

As the investigation unfolds, questions arise about the motives behind the kidnapping. Neighbors have speculated about the possible involvement of a racket, adding a layer of complexity to the case. The police, having identified the individuals involved, are now on the hunt for the kidnappers who sought a ransom of Rs 10 lakh. The ongoing investigations aim to unravel the full extent of the conspiracy and bring those responsible to justice.

Community Response:

The news of Abigail’s abduction prompted an outpouring of support from the local community and beyond. Neighbors expressed deep concern, and a dedicated helpline was established by the police to encourage people to provide any information that could aid in the search. The community’s active involvement and cooperation with law enforcement proved instrumental in the successful resolution of this distressing situation.
