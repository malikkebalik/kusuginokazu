Holliday Grainger Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Holliday Grainger was born on March 27, 1988 in Didsbury, Manchester, England, United Kingdom. She attended University of Leeds where she pursued a degree in English Literature.

At age six, she made her acting debut in the comedy drama series All Quiet on the Preston Front. This led to a number of roles in such television shows as Casualty, Doctors, Dalziel and Pascoe.

As a child actress, she became known for her role as Megan Boothe in Where the Heart Is. In 2011, her breakout role as an adult came in the Showtime series The Borgias, where she plays Lucrezia Borgia.
