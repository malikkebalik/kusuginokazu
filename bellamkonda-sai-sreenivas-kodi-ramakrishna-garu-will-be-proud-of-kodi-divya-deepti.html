Bellamkonda Sai Sreenivas Kodi Ramakrishna Garu will be proud of Kodi Divya Deepti

Bellamkonda Sai Sreenivas – Kodi Ramakrishna Garu will be proud of Kodi Divya Deepti

Published on Sep 15, 2022 1:58 am IST


Young hero Kiran Abbavaram’s Nenu Meku Kavalasinavadini is releasing on a grand note this Friday. Kodi Divya Deepti, the daughter of the late Kodi Ramakrishna Garu is venturing into movie production with the film. The pre-release event was held today and young hero Bellamkonda Sai Sreenivas graced it.

Speaking at the event, Bellamkonda Sai Sreenivas said that he attended the event due to Kodi Divya Deepti. The actor stated that the late Kodi Ramakrishna Garu will be happy and proud to see his daughter enter the film industry.

The actor said that he prays to God to make the film a successful one and added that with the blessings of the late Kodi Ramakrishna Garu the film will emerge a hit. Sreenivas also said that Kiran Abbavaram became very dear to everyone with SR Kalyanamandapam and hoped that this film will become memorable for the whole cast and crew.

The actor also thanked the fans for showering their love. The film is directed by Sridhar Gade of SR Kalyanamandapam fame. Sonal Thakur and Sanjana Anand are the female leads. Mani Sharma is the music composer.
