Matthew Perry Lists LA Penthouse For $35 Million

Friends alum Matthew Perry has evidently had enough of the Los Angeles penthouse he purchased back in 2017, as he has now listed the so-called "Mansion in the Sky" at the top of The Century luxury condominium in Los Angeles for an asking price of $35 million.

Also known by its official designation as Penthouse 40, the property is currently the most expensive condo for sale in all of Los Angeles, per a recent Forbes story. Perry reportedly paid $20 million for the penthouse in 2017, sinking an untold amount of additional money into an extensive renovation over the last couple of years. It measures 9,290 square feet with room for four bedrooms and eight bathrooms, as well as a generous four outdoor terraces, a private screening room, and a fireplace and lounge inside the master suite.

And of course, The Century has plenty of luxury amenities of its own that you would expect from the location of the most expensive condo in LA, like a pool and spa, a gym, a restaurant, its own screening room, and a reported four acres of gardens.

It's a stunning luxury penthouse that might even put Chandler Bing's improbably spacious Manhattan digs to shame. As the official listing describes it:

"Hovering above the city like a floating palace is Penthouse 40, the "Mansion in the Sky" that occupies the entire 40th floor of the exclusive Century building. The sprawling interior has been transformed by architect Scott Joyce and noted interior designer LM Pagano into a stunning palette of textures & colors that are both contemporary & classic, comprising a bold statement worthy of the most discerning buyer."
