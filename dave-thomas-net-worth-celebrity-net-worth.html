What is Dave Thomas' Net Worth?

Dave Thomas is a Canadian actor, comedian, and television writer who has a net worth of $3 million. Dave Thomas was born in St. Catharines, Ontario, Canada in May 1949. As an actor he starred on the television series SCTV from 1976 to 1981. From 1993 to 1998 he starred as Russell Norton on the TV series Grace Under Fire.

Thomas has had recurring roles or voice roles in several TV series including The David Steinberg Show, SCTV Network, The New Show, The Red Green Show, The Legend of Tarzan, Mission Hill, Justice League, Arrested Development, King of the Hill, Bob & Doug, Bounty Hunters, Comedy Bang! Bang! and more. Dave Thomas has also appeared in several films including Stripes, Strang Brew, Coneheads, Pippi Longstocking, Rat Race, Beethoven's 5th, The Aristocrats, and more. He has been nominated for six Primetime Emmy Awards, two Gemini Awards, and an Annie Award.
