Anwar Jibawi Social Network

Timeline

In February 2018, Jibawi directed a music video for Anitta's Vai Malandra (Remix) by Alesso and KO:YU. The video featured Jibawi and friends dancing to the remix in a farm setting.

In November 2018, Jibawi directed the music video for Aitana (singer) and Lele Pon's Teléfono (Remix) which has reached over 35 million views on YouTube.

In June 2018, Jibawi launched a new series, One Star with Anwar, on Instagram's new IGTV platform where he reviews terrible items found online shopping.

Jibawi started his internet career on Vine. He partnered with Shots Studios to create videos and launched his YouTube channel July 2016. In January 2017, Jibawi helped launch Mike Tyson’s YouTube page and he and Tyson have frequently collaborated on one another’s channels. Jibawi also had a role in the movie Keys of Christmas with Rudy Mancuso, Mariah Carey and DJ Khaled. Jibawi is managed by John Shahidi of Shots Studios, a company that also produces his YouTube content. He was invited by Dolce & Gabbana to walk in their Milan Men’s Fashion Week Spring/Summer 2018 show on June 17, 2017.

Anwar Jibawi (born August 9, 1991) is a Palestinian-American internet personality. He is an actor and comedian most notable for his comedy videos on video service YouTube and previously on the Vine app. He has over 5.72 million subscribers on YouTube and over 7.2 million followers on Instagram. Jibawi is managed by John Shahidi of Shots Studios.
