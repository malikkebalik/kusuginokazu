Natalie Found Nudes in Mike's Phone After Engagement

TV

'90 Day Fiancé' couple Mike and Natalie argued again at the Tell All, with Natalie Mordovtseva revealing that she found nudes on Mike Youngquist's phone.

Published on August 22, 2021

2 min read

90 Day Fiancé couple Mike and Natalie were not able to make it work — and frankly, it never seemed likely that the two would stay together, given how they treat one another. That being said, the 90 Day Fiancé Tell All has been a chance for both sides to air grievances. And while Mike Youngquist tried to garner sympathy by mentioning that Natalie Mordovtseva uses his bank card, Natalie had a secret weapon — apparently, she found nudes on Mike’s phone after the couple got engaged. Now, fans feel like Mike’s story doesn’t add up, and they’re blasting Trish for her comments. 

Natalie Mordovtseva says she found nudes

90 Day Fiancé star Natalie Mordovtseva has brought up Mike Youngquist’s naughty photos before. Specifically, during Mike’s birthday dinner, Natalie mentioned that one of his friends kept sending him nudes. 

Mike stood up to leave, indicating it was clearly a sensitive subject. He left Natalie at the hotel, saying he would head home and take shots. 

“What is the reason to stand up and leave if you have nothing to hide?” Natalie asked him. 

 “Michael said that he would like me to be not that aggressive towards his friends and I ask him, could you please not send them naked pictures? A girl from Nevada. He said she’s his friend but she kept sending him her boobs,” Natalie told producers at the time. 

During the Tell All episode, Trish brought up their “terrible” Paris trip, which surprised Natalie — she didn’t think it had been that bad. Mike said they had a lot of big fights, and Natalie claimed it was due to nudes. 

“Because I found naked boobs on his phone,” Natalie replied. She suggested this happened mere hours after their engagement. Natalie claims she found the photos while scrolling through his phone, including his deleted photos. 

‘90 Day Fiancé’ fans don’t think Mike’s story adds up

90 Day Fiancé fans aren’t entirely convinced that Mike Youngquist wasn’t up to no good. Mike claimed that he’d been sent the photos and deleted them previously and that Natalie looked into his deleted files. 

“And first thing what I see is this lady, and I see of course the date. It was one week before he arrived,” Natalie claimed. Fans were with Natalie on this, many suspected that Mike had saved the photos and had been keeping them. 

“No he definitely was talking to his ex and saved those [photos] and then deleted them when he went to see Natalie. But we had not known this info until this Tell All,” wrote one Redditor.

“Mike said those nude pictures were in his deleted photos album and Natalie discovered them on his phone. I totally believe he saved those pictures,” wrote another user. 

Trish tries to deflect blame on Natalie

Mike and Natalie’s discussion of the nudes photos on the 90 Day Fiancé Tell All episode led to Mike’s mother, Trish, adding her two cents. Trish has suspected Natalie Mordovtseva and her intentions for some time now and seems to take any opportunity she can to bring Natalie down. 

Despite Mike’s own admission that he handed Natalie his phone for some reason he can’t recall, she told Natalie that she shouldn’t have been snooping in Mike’s phone — seemingly trying to deflect attention away from Mike’s own indiscretions.
