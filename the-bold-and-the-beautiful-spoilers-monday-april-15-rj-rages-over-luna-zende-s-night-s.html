The Bold and the Beautiful Spoilers: Monday, April 15 RJ Rages Over Luna &amp; Zendes Night S

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers for Monday, April 15, reveal that Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada) will continue her confession and deliver some news that’s going to rock RJ Forrester’s (Joshua Hoffman) world.

Luna admitted she accidentally took Poppy Nozawa’s (Romy Park) special mints, so that’s why she was feeling so off that night.

RJ also suspects something happened after the party at the Forrester mansion, but he doesn’t have the full story yet.

B&B spoilers say that’ll change once Luna spills that she ended up at the guest house, where Zende Forrester Dominguez (Delon de Metz) found her waiting in his bed.

Luna will insist she thought Zende was RJ in her drugged state, so that led to a huge mistake that she’s been wrestling with ever since.

Once RJ realizes Luna and Zende crossed a passionate line together, he’ll fly off the handle. RJ will pull his hand from Luna’s grip as he struggles with some difficult emotions.

Although RJ won’t blame Luna for accidentally getting drugged and hallucinating, he’ll inevitably wonder why she waited so long to come clean.

Luna has been keeping a massive secret, so RJ may ask if it’s because she has real feelings for Zende.

Of course, Luna will insist RJ is the only guy for her and argue that she just didn’t want to blow things up.

However, it reached the point where Luna couldn’t deal with her guilt any longer, so that’s why she’s finally coming clean now.

Luna didn’t cheat willingly, but she still landed in bed with RJ’s cousin – and that’s going to sting.

RJ will ultimately rush out the door and head for Forrester Creations, so that’ll lead to him clobbering Zende over Luna’s revelation soon enough!

In the meantime, Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) will make an angry demand as she fumes over John “Finn” Finnegan’s (Tanner Novlan) story.

Since Steffy doesn’t believe all this “10 toes” stuff, she’ll insist Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) needs to get a grip and accept that Sheila is truly gone.

Steffy might even plan on telling Deacon that to his face eventually, so we’ll bring you more predictions on the hostility that’s still to come.

As for Deacon, he’ll have a hard time figuring out those text messages he discovered.

Deacon doesn’t know who “Sugar” is, but Lauren Fenmore Baldwin (Tracey E. Bregman) will help connect some Sheila lookalike dots later during the week of April 15-19.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Deacon will mull over this Sugar puzzle until then, so stay tuned for updates on his attempt to process everything.
