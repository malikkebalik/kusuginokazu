Where Do Meghan Crumpler and Vannessa Cross Live?

Fans of TLC’s 1000-lb Sisters will certainly enjoy 1000-lb Best Friends. The reality series follows four best friends as they share what their lives are like as they attempt to lose weight. Meghan Crumpler and Vannessa Cross have been friends for years, and they appeared on another reality series prior to this one titled Too Large. So, where do Meghan Crumpler and Vannessa Cross live?

Where do Meghan Crumpler and Vannessa Cross live?

Fans will get to see plenty of Meghan Crumpler and Vannessa Cross’s friendship on 1000-lb Best Friends. Early previews of the show prove the two are close, though Crumpler already had weight loss surgery and Cross has not. So, where do the best friends live?

According to The Cinemaholic, Cross lives in or around Atlanta, Georgia. Vanessa Cross’s Instagram shows a number of posts where she’s in Atlanta — and one from November 2021 suggests she lives there or close by. “This journey isn’t going to be easy, but I know I can do it!” she captioned a post of herself throwing up a peace sign while outdoors. She tagged the location as Atlanta, Georgia, and she also tagged the location in her post.

As for Crumpler, she reportedly lives in Hoschton, Georgia, and she’s from Stockbridge, Georgia. Meghan Crumpler’s Instagram also features photos and videos of her in this location and in Atlanta. It takes about an hour by car to travel from Atlanta to Hoschton.

Meghan Crumpler says she lives in her friend’s basement — and the friend is also featured on ‘1000-lb Best Friends’

Meghan Crumpler shared more information about her living situation. She spoke to TV Insider about 1000-lb Best Friends, and she divulged that she lives in Tina Arnold’s basement apartment. Arnold is another one of the friends featured on the show, as is Ashley Sutton.

“Well, I actually live with Tina in her basement, so we share quite a bit,” Crumpler said in her interview. “I would say the show does feature me and Vannessa because we were on Too Large together, but I would definitely say that Tina and Ashley both are just as amazing as we are, and I think you’ll find that out once you get to see the whole thing unfold. And they’re just amazing women, period.”

Jon, Crumpler’s boyfriend while filming Too Large, proposed to her during the show. It’s unclear what their relationship is like now with 1000-lb Best Friends, but they currently don’t seem to live together.

When does ‘1000-lb Best Friends’ air? How many episodes does the season have?

Viewers excited for the new TLC show can’t wait for it to premiere. 1000-lb Best Friends airs Monday, Feb. 7, 2022, at 10 p.m. EST. New episodes air on Mondays after that.

How many episodes does 1000-lb Best Friends have in the first season? According to Distractify, TLC lists new episodes of the series up until March 21, 2022. This means the season will likely have seven episodes total. 1000-lb Sisters typically had six episodes per season, so seven episodes makes sense for the new spinoff series.

We look forward to learning more about Meghan Crumpler, Vannessa Cross, Tina Arnold, and Ashley Sutton with the new show.
