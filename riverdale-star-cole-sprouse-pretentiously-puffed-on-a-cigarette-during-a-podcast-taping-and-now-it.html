On a divided internet, one recent event has united users: The actor Cole Sprouse smoking indoors, and taking an unnecessarily long drag of his cigarette during a recent interview, is giving people "the ick."

The moment during his "Call Her Daddy" podcast appearance on Wednesday has invited such strong adverse reactions that it's become our newest meme.

After the interview was released, user @nothnghppens shared a viral tweet that featured several clips of the actor holding, and then puffing on, his cigarette as host Alex Cooper asked him a series of questions. People immediately responded with confusion and repulsion.
