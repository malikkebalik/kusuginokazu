What is Oleg Boyko's Net Worth?

Oleg Boyko is a Russian businessman who has a net worth of $1.4 billion. Oleg Boyko was already backed with a degree in economics when he opened the first stores with credit card readers in Moscow in the 1990s. Later on, Boyko has expanded his company into imported fruit, traded food and electronics, and eventually managed banks. But the most of the group's assets were restructured and sold throughout the 1995 banking crisis.

The returns from the sale he reinvested in several industries, i.e., metallurgy, real estate and retail. Today, Boyko owns the investment holding Finstar Financial Group. In 2012, he sold his 51% stake in the Rive Gauche chain, one of Russia's biggest perfume and cosmetics sellers, and has also invested in a company called 4finance that offers micro loans in Eastern Europe and Scandinavia.
