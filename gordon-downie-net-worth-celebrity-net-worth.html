What is Gordon Downie's Net Worth?

Gordon Downie was a Canadian musician, writer, and actor who had a net worth of $10 million. Gordon Downie was born in Amherstview, Ontario, Canada, and grew up in Kingston, Ontario. He met the members of what would become his band, The Tragically Hip, while in school. He co-founded the group in 1983, and they first made a name for themselves as a cover band. They were spotted by record executive, Bruce Dickinson, the President of MCA, and signed with the label soon after.
