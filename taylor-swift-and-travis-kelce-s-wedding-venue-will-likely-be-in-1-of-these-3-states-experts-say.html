Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's Wedding Venue Will Likely Be in 1 of These 3 States, Experts Say

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce made a splash during the first year of their relationship. The pop superstar and the Kansas City Chiefs tight end quickly fell in love after going on a few dates. And fans can’t get enough of their whirlwind romance. So, will wedding bells ring anytime soon for the happy couple? Here’s what betting experts said about where Kelce and Swift may want to get married.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce will likely get married in Rhode Island, betting experts claim

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce remain tight-lipped about their romance. Swift will stay busy with “The Eras Tour” until December 2024, while Kelce has football, his podcast, and new Hollywood endeavors he’s taking on in the world of showbiz. While the couple has a lot going on as individuals, they make time for each other. Kelce loves watching Swift on stage during her tour. And he frequently buys her lavish gifts to remind her just how special she is.

The couple hasn’t publicly discussed engagement or marriage. However, fans think Swift and Kelce plan on staying together forever. So, if the couple gets married, where will they have their wedding? Betting experts from Sportscasting.com think the wedding will either happen in Rhode Island, Tennessee, or New York. According to experts, the couple has a 33% chance of tying the knot in Rhode Island, a 28.6% chance of having the wedding in Tennessee, and a 20% chance of a New York wedding.

Swift has homes in Westerly, Rhode Island, Nashville, Tennessee, and New York City, New York. Betting experts also stated that the couple could throw a curveball and have a wedding in Kelce’s home state of Ohio.

“Kelce’s hometown of Ohio could be an outside option at 16/1, while with Swift having links and homes in New York and Tennessee, these states will be praying they hear wedding bells to get the added attention,” spokesman Andy Newton explained. “However, the word on the street is that Rhode Island, where Swift has a $17 million beach-fronted mansion and grounds, has already been discussed by the pair as their top-favored wedding venue, and so I’m with the bookies here and will side with the smallest U.S. state grabbing all the headlines.”

Another source claims the couple agreed on a wedding venue

Fans have previously heard of Rhode Island as a possible wedding venue for Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce. A source told Life & Style that Swift wants a summer wedding “surrounded by family and close friends” at her place in Rhode Island.

The insider added that the couple would “spare no expense” with a “lavish outdoor ceremony” that Swift had always dreamed of. As for the guest list, the couple plans on inviting their A-list friends.

“There’s talk of banning phones,” the insider continued, “and having everyone sign NDAs to keep the day as private as possible.”

Swift’s 11,000-square-foot Rhode Island estate is on 5.23 acres and contains eight bedrooms, 10 bathrooms, eight fireplaces, a pool, and 700 feet of beachfront.

A source claimed Taylor Swift ‘is already working on her vows’

How serious are Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce regarding their future wedding? Some sources claim the couple plans to wait a while before seriously discussing an engagement. However, a source told In Touch Weekly in January 2024 that the couple became “super serious” after five months. The source claimed that Kelce already got down on one knee for Swift, though the couple continues to keep the engagement private.

“Taylor is already working on her vows,” the source alleged, according to Yahoo. “Knowing her, they’ll eventually become a song!”

“Taylor’s definitely found the one,” they continued. “She’s finally ready to take the plunge and get married.”
