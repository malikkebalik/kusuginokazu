What is Domingo Beckmann's Net Worth?

Domingo Beckmann is a Mexican businessman who has a net worth of $1 billion. Domingo Beckmann is the chief executive officer and Executive of General Direction of Jose Cuervo. He is best known for being part of the Grupo Cuervo which has part ownership in the tequila brand Jose Cuervo. The brand was founded in 1975 and is the best-selling tequila brand in the world.

Jose Cuervo makes up more than one third of the market share both worldwide and in the United States. Beckmann is part of a family of direct descendants of Don Jose Antonio de Cuervo who founded the brand. As of 2013 Jose Cuervo is distributed by Proximo Spirits.
