Hire Adi Oasis for a Corporate Event or Performance Booking.

The French-Caribbean singer-producer just revealed the release date for her upcoming full-length album, Lotus Glow. The album, which marks her debut as Adi Oasis, has a diverse cast of guests, including KIRBY, Leven Kali, Jamila Woods and Aaron Taylor.
On Lotus Glow, the French Caribbean-born, Brooklyn-based soul-funk-R&B singer blends her expert production skills, soaring vocal abilities and stunning bass proficiency to produce her most intimate and political work to date. The political topics are inevitable for Adi.
Adi's journey is summed up in the album's name, Lotus Glow, which also represents her aspirations for the future.
Adi has gained wide-ranging praise from critics and a devoted following since going solo, in part because of her amazing live performances. She has received glowing reviews from the press, including Vogue to Rolling Stone, Wonderland, Line of Best Fit, Afropunk and many more. Her track "Whisper My Name" was included in a spectacular COLORS premiere.
She has played at festivals including Afropunk, Central Park Summerstage, Funk on the Rocks (Red Rocks), London Jazz Fest and more while touring the world and sharing the stage with musicians like Anderson.Paak, Gregory Porter, JUNGLE, Keyshia Cole, Lee Fields and Big Freeda..
