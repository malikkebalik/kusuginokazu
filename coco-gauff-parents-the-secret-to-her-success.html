Coco Gauff Parents: The Secret to Her Success?

While Coco Gauff competes in her first U.S. Open semifinal, her parents, Candi Gauff and Corey Gauff, will be cheering from the player’s box. When Gauff won the Cincinnati Open earlier this summer, she gave a speech in which she thanked her parents. Thank you for always being there for me, Mom. It’s because of my dad that I’m even here. Why I enjoy playing tennis. He’s the inspiration for my self-assurance.

The 19-year-old tennis prodigy credited her parents as “really my support system,” citing their assistance in keeping her organized and on top of her game. My father still sends me weekly scouting reports. My mother’s primary role is that of a mother. My dad, doing what a dad does. Read on to meet Coco Gauff parents.

Coco Gauff Parents

Corey Gauff was born in Delray Beach, Florida, and he attended Georgia State University, where he played collegiate basketball. When he was younger, he dabbled in tennis. Candi Gauff (née Odom) is a native Floridian; she was born to Eddie “Red” Odom and Yvonne Lee Odom in Delray Beach. Her father, Eddie, started the Delray Beach American Little League after playing college football and minor-league baseball.
