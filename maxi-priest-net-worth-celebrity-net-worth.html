What is Maxi Priest's Net Worth?

Maxi Priest is an English reggae singer who has a net worth of $10 million. Maxi Priest was born in Lewisham, London, England in June 1961. He has become one of the most successful reggae fusion singers of all time. Priest released his debut studio album You're Safe in 1985.

He went on to release the albums Intentions in 1986, Maxi in 1988, Bonafide in 1990, Fe Real in 1992, Man with the Fun in 1996, CombiNation in 1999, 2 the Max in 2005, Refused in 2007, and Easy to Love in 2014 which reached #2 on the Billboard Top Reggae Albums chart. Priest's single "Wild World" reached #5 in the UK, Ireland, Belgium, and New Zealand and #3 in Norway. His single "Close to You" reached #1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart and #2 on the US R&B chart as well as #2 in Sweden, Australia, and New Zealand.
