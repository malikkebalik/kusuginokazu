Sean Casten Daughter Autopsy: How Did Gwen Casten Die?

Illinois congressman Sean Casten’s daughter, who was 17 years old, has passed away. On Sunday, June 12, 2022, after spending the night out with friends, Gwen Casten, a recent graduate of Downers Grove North High School in Downers Grove, Illinois, returned home. The following morning, her body was discovered. Why did this occur? When did Gwen pass away, and why?

Sean Casten Daughter Autopsy

Dr. Hilary Strawn McElligott, the hospital’s head pathologist, concluded after months of research that Ms. Casten died from a “sudden arrhythmogenic cardiac event of a natural but unspecified etiology,” which fits the clinical presentation of the patient’s symptoms.

An arrhythmia, also known as an irregular heartbeat, is a condition in which the heart beats excessively rapidly, too slowly, or at an irregular pace, as defined by the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute. Without treatment, arrhythmias can cause a stroke, heart failure, or even death.

Casten reported Gwen’s death that morning on June 13, 2022, via his social media accounts that night. A picture of Gwen and a statement from Casten, Kara, and their daughter Audrey were posted online on June 15th, two days later.
