Gisborne Cruises: Best Cruises to Gisborne, NZ

On a New Zealand cruise, visit the port of Gisborne, a city rich in history and natural beauty. Outdoor activities are popular here; take a refreshing dip in the ocean, surf along the coast, or hike up trails that lead to spectacular views. Discover the country’s indigenous and colonial history at a local museum, and visit St. Mary’s Church to admire its Maori carvings. 

The farms in the area capture this land’s rural charms, where you can watch a sheep-shearing demonstration, or wander through the pine-scented Eastwoodhill Arboretum. Sample local wine at regional vineyards and wineries, or stop by the local brewery and taproom. A cruise to Gisborne is the perfect way to indulge in New Zealand’s culinary, cultural, and natural treasures.
