What is Nassef Sawiris' Net Worth?

Nassef Sawiris is an Egyptian businessman who has a net worth of $6.1 billion dollars. Born in Egypt, Nassef Sawiris currently resides in Cairo, and serves as CEO of Orascom Construction Industries. His father founded Orascom, and Nassef Sawiris began serving as Head of Construction in the early 90s. He took over as CEO in 1998.
