What is Chase Landry's Net Worth?

Chase Landry is an American alligator hunter and reality television personality who has a net worth of $200 thousand. Chase Landry is based in Pierre Part, Louisiana, where, part of each year, he works as an alligator hunter with his family. His father and older brother are also alligator hunters. The members of Landry family are the subjects of the reality series, "Swamp People", which follows various teams of alligator hunters through the swampland as they hunt alligators during the month of September each year.

The show began airing in 2010, and has so far aired four seasons. Each episode follows approximately four teams as they try to make their living by catching alligators in the Atchafalaya River Basin in Louisiana. For the fourth season of the program, the show began following alligator hunters in northern Louisiana, as well. Chase Landry joined the cast as a full member in Season 3.
