What is Mark Driscoll's Net Worth?

Mark Driscoll is an American evangelical Christian pastor and author who has a net worth of $2.5 million. Mark Driscoll was born in Grand Forks, North Dakota in October 1970. Driscoll graduated from Washington State University and Western Seminary. In 1996 he co-founded Mars Hill Church which first met in his home. As of March 2014 Mars Hill Church had 14,000 members located in fifteen locations in five states. Driscoll founded a theological cooperative called The Resurgence and has cofounded the parachurch organizations Churches Helping Churches, The Gospel Coalition, and the church planting Acts 29 Network.

Mark has written for the Seattle Times, Fox News website, and OnFaith. He has also authored several books. He was named by Preaching magazine as one of the 25 most influential pastors of the past 25 years in 2011. Driscoll is a polarizing figure and has been described as a cult of personality and accused of using controversy to increase his visibility. Driscoll has been involved in controversy regarding his views of gender roles, perceived misogyny, Calvinist theory, culture of fear, and plagiarism accusations. Driscoll was expelled by Acts29 in August 2014 for ungodly and disqualifying behavior.
